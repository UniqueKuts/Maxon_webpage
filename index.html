About Maxon Healthcare - Your Partner in Workplace Health

Maxon Healthcare is a healthcare service provider established in 2024. Maxon Healthcare is a dynamic and growing health services provider, dedicated to delivering high-quality, technology-enabled medical care through on-site medical camps, diagnostic services, and digital health solutions. Headquartered in Ongole, Andhra Pradesh, Maxon Healthcare operates with a commitment to preventive healthcare, workplace wellness, and mobile diagnostics.

Our mission: Delivering quality healthcare onsite with precision and care.
Our vision: Making workplace health accessible and impactful.

What services does Maxon Healthcare provide?
